Mexican Rice

This Mexican rice recipe makes a wonderful restaurant-style side dish for any Mexican dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp vegatable oil
  • 1 cup uncooked long-grain rice
  • Garlic salt, to taste
  • Cumin, to taste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 8oz Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Season rice and cook in oil until puffed and golden
  2. Add onion and cook until onion softens
  3. Stir in broth and tomato sauce, bring to boil
  4. Reduce to simmer, cover, cook for 20 mins
