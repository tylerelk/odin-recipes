Mexican Corn

This elote recipe for Mexican corn on the cob was given to me by a Mexican friend of mine. Street vendors there sell their corn made this way.

Ingredients

  • 4 corn cobs
  • Butter and mayonaise
  • Cotija Cheese
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Grill corn until charred on all sides
  2. Roll in butter and spread with mayonaise
  3. Sprinkle with Cotija, squeeze 1/4 lime on top
